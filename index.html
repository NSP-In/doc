NOTICE

All newly admitted students are hereby informed that they must appear for physical document verification on 22nd September 2025 (Monday). The following documents are required to be produced at the time of verification.
  • Class 12 (Higher Secondary) Examination Marksheet – Xerox copy
  • Class 10 (Madhyamik) Examination Marksheet – Xerox copy
  • Birth Certificate – Original and Xerox copy
  • Domicile Certificate – Original and Xerox copy
  • Bank Passbook (front page showing account details) – Xerox copy
  • Admit Card and Rank Card of Entrance Examination – Xerox copies
  • Any other documents as specified in the admission instructions/form
  • 2 recent passport-size colour photographs
Students must bring all the above-mentioned documents on the very date.
